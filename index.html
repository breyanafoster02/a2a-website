About A2A Solutions

Passion
for Growth.

A2A Solutions is an independent advisory consulting firm serving government agencies and mission-driven organizations. We provide senior-level financial management support, program advisory, and analytics solutions — intentionally structured for focused, high-impact delivery.

"We are intentionally structured to deliver focused, senior-level support without unnecessary overhead, helping our clients move from planning to execution with clarity and confidence."

Founded and led by a federal financial management practitioner with 11+ years of DoD experience, A2A Solutions brings the depth of a large firm with the responsiveness and direct access of a boutique practice.
